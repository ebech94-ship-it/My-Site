Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

Forex Tournaments Arena (“the App”, “we”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Account details (name, email, profile image if uploaded)
  • Device and app usage information
  • Tournament performance data (scores, win/loss records)
  • Optional data you choose to provide (bio, avatar, etc.)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide access to tournaments and gameplay features
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Personalize user experience
  • Ensure compliance with rules and maintain fair gameplay
  • Communicate important updates and notifications

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using encrypted and industry-standard methods. We take reasonable steps to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or alteration of your data.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. Data may only be shared in these limited cases:

  • When required by law
  • To protect the rights and safety of users
  • To enforce Terms & Conditions
  • With service providers strictly necessary for app functionality

6. Use of Synthetic Charts

The candles and charts in this App are synthetic and do not reflect real market data. No personal data is linked to real financial activity.

7. User Rights

  • Delete your account
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request a summary of your stored data

8. Cookies & Tracking

The App may use simple analytics and device identifiers to improve performance. No invasive tracking is used.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Third-Party Services

Some features may link to third-party libraries (e.g., authentication or analytics). Their privacy practices are separate and governed by their own policies.

Please note that this app does not collect financial trading data or execute real-money trades. All competitions are simulated.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated terms.

12. Contact Us

For concerns or inquiries about this Privacy Policy, visit the Help Center inside the App or contact our support email.
