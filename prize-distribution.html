Tournament Rewards & Prize Distribution

FX Arena Competition Rules

1. Tournament Types

FX Arena offers two main tournament types:

  • Demo Tournaments – Free practice competitions with no entry fee.
  • Paid Tournaments – Competitions that require a small entry fee to participate.

2. Entry Fees

Paid tournaments require an entry fee before participation. The entry fee contributes to the tournament prize pool and helps maintain the competition structure.

3. Prize Pool Formation

The prize pool of a paid tournament is formed primarily from the total entry fees collected from participants.

A small portion may also be allocated for platform maintenance, system operations, and tournament administration.

4. Winner Determination

Winners are determined based on the final tournament account balance at the end of the competition period.

Traders who achieve the highest balances rank higher on the leaderboard and qualify for reward distribution.

5. Prize Distribution

At the end of each tournament, rewards are distributed among the top-ranking participants according to the predefined prize structure displayed before the tournament begins.

The prize allocation may vary depending on the number of participants and the total prize pool available.

6. Fair Competition

All participants begin tournaments with the same starting conditions, including equal starting balances and the same synthetic market environment.

This ensures that results are determined purely by strategy, discipline, and trading performance.

7. Transparency

FX Arena aims to maintain transparent competition rules. Tournament details such as entry fee, prize pool, number of winners, and reward distribution are displayed before participants join a tournament.

8. Disclaimer

FX Arena tournaments are based on simulated trading environments. They are designed for entertainment, education, and competitive gameplay purposes.

The platform does not provide real brokerage services or real financial trading.
