Understanding Market Psychology

Market Psychology Matters

The market is driven by human emotion — fear, greed, hope, and uncertainty.

Traders buy when they anticipate price growth and sell when they expect decline.

In FX Arena tournaments, strategy, discipline, and timing determine success.

How To Participate

1. Register

2. Fund Wallet

Deposit funds to your real account. Premium tournament registration fees will be deducted from it.

3. Tournament Balance

Each tournament assigns a starting balance to all participants. Grow this balance to climb the leaderboard.

4. Join Tournament

Select an available tournament and register. Then switch to the tournament account to begin trading.

5. Climb Rankings

Profitable trades increase your balance and move you up the leaderboard. Losses reduce your ranking.
